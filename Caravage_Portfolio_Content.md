**Strategic Value**

## Portfolio Strategy

### Investment Themes

Our portfolio reflects concentrated conviction across four interconnected themes:

**1. Web3 Infrastructure**  
Eldora, Drumee, and Chateau Shi represent our belief that blockchain technology will fundamentally reshape asset ownership, digital identity, and value exchange. We focus on infrastructure plays—picks and shovels—rather than speculative tokens.

**2. Heritage Craftsmanship**  
Guillotine Vodka and Eza Watches embody tangible luxury with authentic provenance. In an age of mass production and digital ephemera, genuine craftsmanship commands enduring value.

**3. Fintech Innovation**  
Greengage captures the transformation of financial services—combining regulatory compliance with technological innovation to serve underserved market segments.

**4. Experiential Assets**  
Chateau Shi and related investments recognize the premium consumers place on exclusive experiences and access—economics of scarcity in an abundant world.

### Selection Criteria

Every portfolio company meets our rigorous standards:

- **Experienced Founders**: Deep domain expertise and demonstrated execution capability
- **Differentiated Positioning**: Clear competitive advantages and moats
- **Aligned Incentives**: Management teams with significant personal stakes
- **Tangible Value Creation**: Businesses producing real products, services, or cash flows
- **Multi-Generational Potential**: Sustainable models with decades-long runway

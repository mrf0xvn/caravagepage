Where the Name Comes From

Caravage was founded on a simple yet profound belief: investing is both an art and a science. Like the master painters who balanced technical precision with creative vision, we combine rigorous analysis with strategic intuition.

Our name draws inspiration from Caravaggio, the revolutionary Baroque painter known for his dramatic use of light and shadow — chiaroscuro. In investing, we seek to illuminate opportunities that others overlook, finding value in the contrast between perception and reality.

Just as Caravaggio transformed painting by seeing what others missed, we strive to identify asymmetric opportunities in markets where conventional wisdom falls short.

Our Philosophy

Multi-Generational Thinking

We don't invest for quarters; we invest for generations. Our decisions are measured in decades, not months.

This long-term horizon allows us to weather short-term volatility, compound returns over time, make strategic decisions unconstrained by quarterly pressures, and build lasting value for future generations.

Weather Short-Term Volatility

Patient capital means we're never forced sellers. We hold through cycles and benefit where others capitulate.

Compound Returns Over Time

Time in the market, not timing the market. Consistent, compounding returns across asset classes and cycles.

Unconstrained Decision-Making

No quarterly earnings pressure means we pursue the best opportunities, not the most immediately visible ones.

Build Lasting Value

Every investment decision is made with future generations in mind — the ultimate test of true conviction.

## 📖 ABOUT US PAGE

### Our Story

**Title**: The Art of Investing

**Content**:  
Caravage was founded on a simple yet profound belief: investing is both an art and a science. Like the master painters who balanced technical precision with creative vision, we combine rigorous analysis with strategic intuition.

Our name draws inspiration from Caravaggio, the revolutionary Baroque painter known for his dramatic use of light and shadow—chiaroscuro. In investing, we seek to illuminate opportunities that others overlook, finding value in the contrast between perception and reality.

### Our Philosophy

**Multi-Generational Thinking**  
We don't invest for quarters; we invest for generations. Our decisions are measured not in months, but in decades. This long-term horizon allows us to:

- Weather short-term volatility  
- Compound returns over time  
- Make strategic decisions unconstrained by quarterly pressures  
- Build lasting value for future generations

**The Caravage Difference**  
What sets us apart:

- **High-Conviction Approach**: Concentrated portfolios of our best ideas, not diversification for its own sake
- **Alignment of Interests**: We invest alongside our partners, with significant personal capital at stake
- **Rigorous Due Diligence**: Every opportunity undergoes exhaustive analysis—financial, legal, operational, and strategic
- **Operational Expertise**: We don't just allocate capital; we actively work to enhance value

## 🎯 INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY PAGE

### Our Investment Criteria

**Title**: How We Invest

**The Four Filters**:

Every investment opportunity must pass through four rigorous filters:

1. **Alignment** — Does this align with our multi-generational wealth preservation objectives?

2. **Conviction** — Do we have genuine high conviction, or is this merely "diversification"?

3. **Asymmetry** — Is there a favorable risk/reward profile with limited downside and meaningful upside?

4. **Operational Alpha** — Can we add value beyond capital through expertise, network, or operational improvements?

### Asset Class Focus

**Real Estate**  
Our real estate strategy spans the risk spectrum:

- **Core**: Stabilized, income-generating properties in prime locations
- **Value-Add**: Properties with clear paths to enhanced value through active management
- **Opportunistic**: Development projects and special situations with asymmetric return potential

**Alternative Investments**  
Selective exposure to private market opportunities:

- Private equity and growth capital
- Private credit and specialty finance
- Infrastructure and real assets
- Venture and early-stage (selective)
